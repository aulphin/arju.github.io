    • Biography

    Arju Bano is a Research Scholar in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology(Indian School of Mines) IIT(ISM), Dhanbad. She is working on Deep Learning(DL)/ Machine Learning(ML) Model for real life applications which is helpful to perform Medical Diagnosis cost effective and more efficient led by Dr. Monidipa Das, Assistant Professor at IIT(ISM), Dhanbad.

    Arju Bano is also associated with (NVCTI Lab), NARESH VASHISHT CENTRE FOR TINKERING & INNOVATION affiliated with the Department of Computer Science And Engineering at IIT(ISM), Dhanbad regarding her "IOT(Internet of Things) based Fall Detection" Project done under Dr. Tarachand Amgoth, Asssociate Professor at IIT(ISM) Dhanbad.

    Arju received her master's (M.Tech) degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Compucom Institute Of Technology & Mgmt(CITM) affiliated to Rajasthan Technical University(RTU) in 2021. She received her bachelor's (B.Tech) degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Compucom Institute Of Technology & Mgmt (CITM) Jaipur, India, in 2018.

    She is currently working in the domain of Digital HealthCare.
